More on how it works

Think of wynno as a personalized spam filter for Twitter.

ONCE YOU FOLLOW MORE THAN A FEW PEOPLE ON TWITTER, YADA YADA YADA

wynno divides your Twitter timeline into two timelines, one with all the tweets we think you'll like (The Good Stuff), and one with the stuff you won't (The Rest). But taste is unique to each person; how can wynno guess which tweets you will like?

You train it, by voting or on tweets. Voting teaches our algorithms about your preferences. The more you vote, the better wynno will get at identifying all the tweets you like, and excluding all the ones you'd have been just as happy to miss.

You can also make sure you never miss tweets from certain people, or that contain certain words, by adding those users/words to your megaphone list. Megaphoned tweets always show up in The Good Stuff. Conversely, you can mute certain users or words--these tweets go straight to The Rest.
