Welcome to wynno!

Getting the most out of wynno:

Heard / Muted lists

Use these lists to tell wynno how to handle tweets from certain users or that contain certain keywords. Find them under Settings.

Add to these lists early and often!

As you find yourself voting Yes to all those tweets from your best friend, add him/her to your list of Heard users.

If you're voting No all the time to tweets about Congress, or from that former coworker of yours whom you don't want to unfollow, add them to Muted.

Voting

Think of your vote on a tweet as the answer to:

Do I want to see tweets like this in the future?

Yes
No

'like this' means whatever you want it to--wynno's job is to figure that out.

Three principles:

  1. The more you vote, the better wynno will get.
  2. But don't feel you should vote on every tweet. If a tweet seems like a toss-up, skip it.
  3. Keep adding to your Heard / Muted lists!
