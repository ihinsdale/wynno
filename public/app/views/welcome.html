1. Create filters to Hear or Mute tweets on the Settings page.

Click the and buttons to train wynno to identify what you like and don't like. wynno will suggest filters for you to adopt, and if you want, can even filter out tweets it predicts you won't like which aren't already handled by your filters. Think of your vote on a tweet as the answer to: Do I want to see tweets like this in the future? The more you vote, the better wynno will get at figuring out what you mean by "like this".
