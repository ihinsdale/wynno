Using wynno

1. Filters

Hear or Mute tweets according to your own rules.

Create tweet filters on the Settings page.

2. Training wynno

wynno can suggest filters for you to adopt, and if you want can even filter out tweets it predicts you won't like. You just have to train it to identify what you like and don't like, by clicking the and buttons. The more you vote, the better wynno will get. Think of your vote on a tweet as the answer to:

Do I want to see tweets like this in the future?

'like this' means whatever you want it to--wynno's job is to figure that out.
